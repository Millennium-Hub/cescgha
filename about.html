Our founding

The Chamber is a product of the merger between the two trade associations; Ghana Oil and Gas Service Providers Association(GOGSPA) and Upstream Petroleum Service Providers Association Ghana(UPSPAG) who have been in existence in the past years. This new journey began in 2023 when these two associations came together and decided it was time to merge and create a unified and formidable force to benefit the entire industry and contribute to its growth therefore, incorporated in May 2023. On Thursday, 24th August 2023, the chamber was officially launched at the Petroleum Commission.

Growth & beyond

At Consolidated Energy Services Chamber Ghana(CESCGHA), we are passionate about contributing to the growth and development of the energy sector.

Our Vision

To strengthen the competitiveness of indigenous companies in the energy sector, train and build human resource capacity for members and to work closely with all stakeholders in the industry to improve the energy sector.

Our Objectives

  • To establish a strong Ghanaian energy group responsible for lobbying and promoting its members in Ghana, Africa and international platforms.
  • To maintain and enforce high ethical standards and conduct amongst energy service providers.
  • To protect the interest of members and advocate for the prioritization of Ghanaian service providers in the industry.
  • To provide a stage for members to engage regulatory bodies in educating, advising and developing strategies for the growth of the industry
  • To organize training programs, seminars and conferences to upgrade the skills and knowledge of members for their effective participation in the industry.
  • To establish good strategies to promote local content and influence the implementation of local content laws.
  • To strive for the development of indigenously owned businesses to take on major roles in the value chain of the industry.
  • To offer great networking opportunities for its registered members.
  • To provide a platform for members to form and establish strong partnerships and collaboration for increased capacity
