Meet Our Executive Board

The executive board of CESCGHA

The Executive Board has the ultimate authority to take decisions and make policies affecting the Chamber. It is made up of the the Chairman; Mr. Nuertey Adzeman, the CEO; Mr. Emmanuel Kwabena Tetteh Hazel and 6 board members in the persons of Professor Vera Fiador, Mr. Ransford Kobina Arthur, Mrs. Dela Quarshie-Twum, Mr. Kwame Osei-Sarpong, Mr. Kinsgley Korankye and Mr. Frederick Hesse-Tetteh.

OUR CHAIRMAN-MR. NUETEY ADZEMAN
Mr. Nuertey Adzeman
Chairman
Mr. Nuertey Adzeman has over twenty (20) years of managerial leadership experience of which ten (10) years was spent in the engineering industry in Nigeria. Mr.Adzeman began his career at Fung Tai Engineering Company Limited in Nigeria as country representative for Ghana. He also became country representative for JKL Energy Limited of Aberdeen, United Kingdom in the year 2010. He was a founding member of the Ghana Oil and Gas Services Providers Association (GOGSPA) and served as the Executive Director of GOGSPA from 2009 to 2022. He has an extensive private sector experience in various capacities and has attended several conferences and seminars on oil and gas and marine. Mr. Nuertey Adzeman serves on the local content committee of the Petroleum Commission, representing the indigenous service companies in the Offshore Oil and Gas industry of Ghana.
