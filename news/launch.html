Consolidated Energy Services Chamber officially launched in Accra

September 12, 2023

CESCGHA serves as both a representative body and a platform for oil and gas service providers operating within Ghana's upstream oil and gas sector. Its primary objective is to enhance the competitive edge of local Ghanaian enterprises in the energy industry within Ghana and advocate for its members' interests not only in Africa but also on a global scale within the energy sector.

The establishment of CESCGHA resulted from the merger between the Ghana Oil and Gas Service Providers Association (GOGSPA) and the Upstream Petroleum Service Providers Association (UPSAG). Mr. Nuertey Adzeman, the Chairman of CESCGHA, explained the Chamber's role, highlighting its mission to create an environment conducive to fostering the energy industry's growth.

He expressed confidence that the Chamber's formation would enable them to contribute to industry development through collaboration and innovation, ultimately creating opportunities for economic expansion. As the Guest of Honour at the event, Mr. Egbert Faibille Jnr., CEO of the Petroleum Commission, congratulated CESCGHA on the merger. He pledged the Commission's readiness to engage with the Chamber and provide support to its members whenever necessary.

Mr. Faibille Jnr. remarked, "Congratulations on launching your Chamber. I believe this merger will encourage more Indigenous Ghanaian Companies (IGCs) to join, learn best practices, including competitive bidding, and strategically position themselves to seize industry opportunities." He further encouraged CESCGHA to maintain high professional standards, envisioning them as a future reference point in energy chamber advocacy.The Board Members of CESCGHA comprise Mr. Nuertey Adzeman (Chairman), Professor Vera Fiador, Mrs. Dela Quarshie-Twum, Kingsley Kofi Korankye, Ransford Kobina Arthur, and Kwame Osei-Sarpong.

CESCGHA

To strengthen the competitiveness of indigenous companies in the energy sector, train and build human resource capacity for members and to work closely with all stakeholders in the industry to improve the energy sector.
